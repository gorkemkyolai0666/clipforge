- DIRECT EVOLUTIONARY VARIANT MANDATE: You are strictly commanded to build a direct alternative variant of a globally successful, proven tech giant (e.g., Notion, Stripe, Trello, Slack, Tinder). 
- Do NOT build abstract or unproven software concepts. Retain the core viral mechanics of the predecessor but inject exactly three (3) high-utility feature enhancements, vastly superior Turkish UI/UX simplicity, and friction-free user journeys.
- Pure visual copies or lazy feature-replications without structural usability optimization are strictly forbidden.

- Products must be feasible for a medium-sized team.

- Products must be suitable for parallel development by multiple agents.

- Monetization must be clearly defined for:

  - short term
  - medium term
  - long term

- Study successful competitors' monetization models before deciding pricing.

- Every project must have a sustainable business model.

- Every idea must pass market validation before implementation.

- Building the product must be more valuable than not building it.
